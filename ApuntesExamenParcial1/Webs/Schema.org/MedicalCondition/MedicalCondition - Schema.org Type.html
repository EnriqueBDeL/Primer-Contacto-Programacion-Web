MedicalCondition

A Schema.org Type
Thing > MedicalEntity > MedicalCondition
  • Canonical URL: https://schema.org/MedicalCondition
  • Check for open issues.
Any condition of the human body that affects the normal functioning of a person, whether physically or mentally. Includes diseases, injuries, disabilities, disorders, syndromes, etc.

Example notes or example HTML without markup.
  1. <h1>Stable angina (angina pectoris)</h1>
  2. Stable angina is a medical condition that affects the heart. The
  3. most common causes are atherosclerosis and spasms of the
  4. epicardial artery. Typical symptoms include:
  5. <ul>
  6.   <li>chest discomfort</li>
  7.   <li>feeling of tightness, heaviness, or pain in the chest</li>
  8. </ul>
  9. Both stable angina and a heart attack result in chest pain, but
  10. a heart attack will have chest pain lasting at least
  11. 10 minutes at rest, repeated episodes of chest pain at rest
  12. lasting 5 or more minutes, or an accelerating pattern of chest
  13. discomfort (episodes that are more frequent, severe,
  14. longer in duration, and precipitated by minimal exertion).
  15. <br>
  16. Risk factors for stable angina include:
  17. <ul>
  18.   <li>Age</li>
  19.   <li>Gender</li>
  20.   <li>Systolic blood pressure</li>
  21.   <li>Smoking</li>
  22.   <li>Total cholesterol and/or cholesterol:HDL ratio</li>
  23.   <li>Coronary artery disease</li>
  24. </ul>
  25. The initial treatment for stable angina is usually drug therapy
  26. with aspirin, beta blockers, ACE inhibitors, and/or nitroglycerine.
  27. Future episodes of stable angina can sometimes be prevented by
  28. stopping smoking, weight management, and increased physical activity.
